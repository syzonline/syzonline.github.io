I've got my Ph.D degree at the University of Science and Technology of China and I'm pursuing a post Ph.D position now. I am also a collaborative researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, and a member at China Competer Federation(CCF).

My research interests include computer vision(especially object detection), multimodal representation, cross-modality fundamental model, and machine learning theories. I have published papers in leading international conferences such as International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks, The Pacific Rim International Conference Series on Artificial Intelligence and recent work on International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval is still under review. I am also a member of program committee in China Computer Federation.
